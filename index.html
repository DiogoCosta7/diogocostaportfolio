Portfolio

As a 3D modeler/Industrial designer at Herdmar, I've designed stamping molds for brands like Nespresso, HM Home, Zara Home, Monoware and Sargadelos.
I have also developed molds for Herdmar's new cutlery models and reverse-engineered existing designs to make them suitable for CNC machining.
